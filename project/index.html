A new way to strategize study is here.

Memo organizes research into a cohesive structure. It removes the friction between capturing information and synthesizing it into a usable strategy.

Start Now

The Bridge Between Creative Input and Active Recall.

Memo removes the extra work of preparing to study. When you write in the 'Create' section, the app automatically organizes your thoughts into the right format for the 'Study' section. This means you can move from taking notes to testing your memory without having to reorganize, copy, or paste anything.

A System That Connects Your Knowledge.

Memo doesn't just store notes; it builds a network. Every card you create is automatically linked to related topics within your library. This allows you to see the bigger picture and discover connections between different subjects that you might have missed in a traditional folder system.
